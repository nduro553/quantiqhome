The QuantiqHome Story

From a single neighborhood to a regional household name — here's how we grew.

2009 — The Beginning

A Neighborhood Idea Takes Root

Marcelline Hartwick decorated six homes on her street in Westfield after neighbors kept asking who did her lights. She and Dov formalized the business with a pickup truck, two ladders, and a genuine passion for making homes shine. That first season, they decorated 14 homes and received zero complaints — only referrals.

2011 — First Commercial Contract

Expanding Beyond Residential

The Ridgewood Commons shopping center approached QuantiqHome after seeing their residential work featured in a local lifestyle magazine. The 12-storefront installation required a full week and a crew of five — and it became an annual tradition that continues to this day. Commercial work now represents 35% of our annual revenue.

2014 — Design Studio Opens

Bringing Creativity In-House

We opened our first dedicated design studio and showroom, allowing clients to preview color palettes, ornament collections, and lighting configurations before installation day. The studio also became home to our custom wreath and garland workshop, where our artisans craft bespoke greenery pieces using locally sourced materials.

Eco-Friendly Decorating Initiative

QuantiqHome became one of the first holiday decorating companies in the region to achieve eco-certification. We transitioned our entire lighting inventory to energy-efficient LED technology, introduced biodegradable packaging for all stored decorations, and partnered with local tree farms that practice sustainable harvesting.

Resilience Through Challenging Times

When the world slowed down, people invested more in their homes — and QuantiqHome was there to help. We introduced contactless consultation via video call, expanded our storage facility, and hired six additional decorators to meet unprecedented demand. 2020 became our highest-volume year to date.

Serving 3,200+ Happy Clients

Today, QuantiqHome operates with a team of 28 specialists, a fleet of six fully equipped service vehicles, and a 4,000 sq ft design studio and storage facility. Holiday Decorating Tips

Expert Advice From Our Decorating Team

Our specialists share their best tips, tricks, and insights to help you get the most out of your holiday decorating — whether you hire us or go the DIY route.

Lighting

Why LED Lights Are the Only Choice for Modern Holiday Displays

LED holiday lights use up to 80% less energy than traditional incandescent bulbs, last 10x longer, and produce far less heat — making them safer for use near greenery and fabrics. Our team exclusively uses commercial-grade LED strands rated for outdoor use in temperatures as low as -40°F. When selecting LEDs, look for strands with a minimum IP65 weather resistance rating and a color rendering index (CRI) above 80 for the most vibrant, true-to-life colors.

Ask our team about LED options →
Greenery

How to Keep Fresh Wreaths and Garlands Looking Beautiful All Season

Fresh greenery can last 4-6 weeks with proper care. The key is moisture — mist your wreaths and garlands with water every 2-3 days, especially in heated indoor environments. Keep them away from direct heat sources like radiators and fireplaces. For outdoor pieces, a light coating of anti-desiccant spray (available at garden centers) can extend freshness by sealing in moisture. Our artisans also recommend choosing varieties like noble fir, incense cedar, and white pine, which naturally retain moisture longer than other species.

Design

Choosing a Holiday Color Palette That Works With Your Home's Existing Décor

The most common decorating mistake is choosing holiday colors that clash with your home's permanent furnishings. Start by identifying your home's dominant neutral tones — warm beiges and creams pair beautifully with gold, copper, and burgundy accents, while cool grays and whites work best with silver, blue, and icy white palettes. If your home features bold accent walls or colorful upholstery, opt for a more restrained holiday palette that complements rather than competes. Our design consultants can walk you through this process during your free consultation.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with QuantiqHome. Can't find your answer here? Give us a call or send us a message.

When should I book my holiday decorating appointment?

We recommend booking as early as possible — ideally by September or October for November and early December installation dates. Our schedule fills up quickly, especially for the Thanksgiving weekend and the first two weeks of December. That said, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests when our schedule allows. Contact us to check current availability.

Do I need to be home during the installation?

For exterior-only installations, you don't need to be present — we just need access to an outdoor electrical outlet. For interior work, we do ask that an adult be home during the installation. Many clients find it fun to be present and watch the transformation unfold, but we understand if you have other commitments and can work around your schedule.

What happens if lights stop working during the season?

All of our installations come with season-long support. If any lights malfunction or decorations are damaged by weather, simply call our support line and we'll dispatch a technician — typically within 24 hours. We carry replacement inventory for all products we install, so repairs are usually completed on the same visit.

Can I use my own decorations, or do I have to use yours?

Absolutely — we're happy to work with your existing decorations. Many clients have cherished ornaments, heirloom pieces, or specific items they want incorporated into the design. Our team will assess your existing inventory during the consultation and integrate it seamlessly with any new pieces we recommend. We can also source specific items you have in mind.

How long does a typical installation take?

Most residential exterior installations take 3-6 hours depending on the size of the property and complexity of the design. Full interior and exterior packages for a standard single-family home typically take one full day with a crew of 2-3 specialists. Commercial installations vary widely based on scope and are scheduled accordingly.

Do you offer services for condos and apartments?

Yes! We offer interior-focused packages specifically designed for condos, apartments, and townhomes. These typically include tree setup and decoration, entryway styling, mantel and window treatments, and tabletop arrangements. We work within HOA guidelines and building regulations to ensure everything is compliant.

What areas do you serve?

QuantiqHome currently serves Union County, Essex County, Morris County, Somerset County, and Middlesex County in New Jersey. We also serve select areas in northeastern Pennsylvania. If you're unsure whether we cover your location, please contact us — we occasionally take on projects outside our standard service area for larger commercial clients.
